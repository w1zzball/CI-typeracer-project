How to Use Typeracer
  1. Select your difficulty level from the dropdown menu
  2. Start typing in the text area to begin the timer
  3. Your typing will be checked in real-time:
    • Green words are correct
    • Red words are incorrect
    • Gray words are not typed yet
  4. Press Enter when you're finished typing
  5. View your results: time taken and words per minute
  6. Click Retry to start a new test
